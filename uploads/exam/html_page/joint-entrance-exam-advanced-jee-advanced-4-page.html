About JEE Advanced

Joint Entrance Examination -Advanced (JEE Advanced ) formerly known as the IIT-JEE. JEE Advanced was started 60 years ago in the year 1961. The exam is conducted to offer admission into the various courses in the IITs like Engineering, Science and Architecture. The JEE Advanced exam in two shifts and each paper for 3 hours. So the total duration of the JEE Advanced exam is 6 hours a day. Its conducted once a year in English and Hindi language. JEE Advanced permits only maximum of 2 attempts in a consecutive year.

Exam pattern

The JEE Advanced exam is conducted in online mode or computer-based mode with two compulsory papers- Paper 1 and Paper 2. Both the papers have three sections i.e Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. From the latest update, the JEE Advanced 2022 paper will contain multiple-choice questions with a single answer correct and double answer correct, numerical value questions along matching type questions. The JEE Advanced exam follows the concept of full, partial, and zero marks. Best CBSE School in Gorakhpur provides daily practice papers on the basis of JEE Advanced pattern.

The JEE Advanced syllabus is slightly different from the JEE Mains syllabus. JEE Advanced generally avoids memorizing topics and follow up with conceptual and application-based topics.
